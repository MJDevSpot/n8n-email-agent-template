## How It Works

```
1. Lead emails your inbox
        ↓
2. AI reads and understands context
        ↓
3. Checks your calendar (all of them)
        ↓
4. Drafts professional response with specific times
        ↓
5. Sends reply (or holds for your review)
        ↓
6. Lead picks a time
        ↓
7. Meeting auto-booked with video link
        ↓
8. You show up and close the deal
```

The entire process takes seconds, not hours.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Will it sound robotic?

No. The AI is trained on your specific business context, services, and communication style. Responses sound natural and professional.

### What if it makes a mistake?

For sensitive topics like pricing or contracts, the system is configured to suggest scheduling a call rather than guessing. All responses are logged so you can review patterns and adjust as needed.

### Can I review emails before they send?

Yes. You can choose:
- Auto-send all responses
- Review all before sending
- Auto-send with notification (recommended)

### What about emails that need a human touch?

The system classifies each email. Complex requests, complaints, or topics outside its scope are flagged for your direct attention.

### How long until it's live?

Typical deployment is 2-3 days from kickoff. Most of that time is waiting for you to provide access and review the AI configuration.

### What if I need to change my availability?

Contact us anytime. Schedule changes are typically implemented same-day.
